Paraná, Brazil

In Paraná, Brazil, the landscape is often marked by Araucaria pine trees, especially the Paraná pine (Araucaria angustifolia), a native species known for its unique umbrella shape. These trees support local wildlife and provide pinhão, a traditional food enjoyed in the region.
