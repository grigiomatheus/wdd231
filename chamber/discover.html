Londrina City

Londrina is a city located in the northern region of the state of Paraná, Brazil. It is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and economic significance. Londrina is a major urban center and a hub for agriculture, commerce, and education in the region. The city offers a variety of attractions, including parks, museums, and cultural events, making it a great place to visit and explore.
